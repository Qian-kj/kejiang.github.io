Hi! I am a Ph.D. student specializing in Computer Science Research at King’s College London. My research contains a range of areas:

  • Computational Social Science
  • Multiagent Systems
  • Network Science
  • Spatio-Temporal Data Mining
  • Geographic Information Science
  • Political Science

These fields find application in several key areas, including Urban Planning, Urban Mobility, Public Health, and Online Communities (particularly in DAO, Decentralized Autonomous Organizations).
